1

Formal Education

Minimum High School Diploma or GED.

2

Training

78 hours of mandatory, standardized recovery specialist training.

3

Lived Experience

You must have at least 18 months of continuous, personal, lived recovery experience. You must also write a minimum 1000 word "Statement of Lived Experience" essay describing your experience with recovery and your history of sustained recovery.

4

Exam

Once your application is approved, you must pass the PCB Examination for Certified Recovery Specialist (CRS examination).
