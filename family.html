Family Resources and Supports

A service of the Region 1 and 4 Recovery Hubs

Dedicated to providing support and resources for families affected by substance use disorder in Philadelphia (Region 1) and Berks, Bucks, Chester, Delaware, Lancaster, Montgomery, and Schuylkill counties (Region 4).

Why Expansion of CFRS and Family Resources is a Priority

Our comprehensive research, including focus groups, surveys, and needs assessments across Region 1 and Region 4, consistently highlighted a significant gap in family support, available resources, and the number of trained professionals specializing in family recovery.

Families shared their urgent need for more educational opportunities to better understand their loved oneâ€™s addiction, its impact on the family system, and effective strategies for support and self-care.

Provider surveys revealed that very few facilities within our service regions offer consistent and ongoing family support programming, leaving a critical need unmet.

Our Goal: Strengthening Family Support

Increase Family Groups and Individual Support

A primary goal is to significantly increase the availability of both family support groups and individual support services throughout Region 1 and Region 4. This will provide families with crucial peer connections and personalized guidance.

Increase Certified Family Recovery Specialists

We are committed to increasing the number of Certified Family Recovery Specialists (CFRS) in our regions. These trained professionals are vital in providing specialized support and guidance to families navigating the complexities of substance use disorder.
